Charity Navigator has awarded IFAW a score of 97 out of 100 for Accountability and Transparency.

Since 1969, the International Fund for Animal Welfare (IFAW) has had a strong vision: a world where animals and people thrive together. From rescuing elephants in Zambia to restoring Australian habitats ravaged by bushfires, wherever there are animals in need, IFAW is there.

Your donation goes directly to where it is needed most, from rapid disaster response and rescue to working with local and federal governments to change policies. Thank you for joining this global community.
